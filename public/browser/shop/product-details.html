Product Description

Our Organic Free-Range Eggs come from chickens that are raised on certified organic farms across Zimbabwe. These chickens enjoy unrestricted outdoor access, allowing them to roam freely in natural pastures where they forage for insects, grasses, and seeds, supplemented with organic feed.

The result is eggs with vibrant, golden-orange yolks that are rich in nutrients and superior in taste. Each egg is carefully collected, inspected for quality, and packed with care to ensure freshness from farm to table.

Why Choose Free-Range Organic?

Free-range organic eggs offer superior nutritional benefits compared to conventional eggs. Studies show they contain up to 7 times more beta carotene, twice as much omega-3 fatty acids, and higher levels of vitamins A and E. The natural diet and lifestyle of free-range chickens result in eggs that are not only healthier but also more flavorful.

Quality Assurance

Every batch of Farm Fresh SA eggs undergoes rigorous quality control. Our farms are regularly inspected and certified by leading organic and animal welfare organizations. We guarantee that our chickens are raised without antibiotics, hormones, or artificial additives.

Storage Instructions

Refrigeration Required

Store eggs in the refrigerator at 4°C or below to maintain freshness and safety.

Storage Guidelines
  • Temperature Keep refrigerated at 1-4°C at all times
  • Placement Store in original carton on a middle or bottom shelf
  • Shelf Life Best within 28 days from pack date
  • Do Not Freeze Freezing in shell can cause quality deterioration
  • After Opening Use within 3-5 days once cracked
Freshness Tips
  • Always check the best-before date on the carton
  • Store eggs in their original carton to prevent moisture loss
  • Do not wash eggs before storing (natural protective coating)
  • Perform float test if uncertain: fresh eggs sink in water
  • Look for clear, thick egg whites and firm, high yolks
Safety Guidelines
  • Do not consume eggs with cracked shells
  • Cook eggs thoroughly until whites and yolks are firm
  • Wash hands and utensils after handling raw eggs
  • Refrigerate leftover egg dishes within 2 hours
  • Do not leave eggs at room temperature for extended periods
