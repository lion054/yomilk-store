Delivery Policy

Important information about our delivery service

What Happens If You're Not Home?

  • Our driver will attempt to contact you via the phone number provided at checkout
  • If you're not available, we'll leave your order in a safe place as arranged
  • For secure locations, we'll request your gate code or building access details
  • We can arrange delivery to a trusted neighbor if you authorize it
  • Rescheduled deliveries can be arranged at no extra cost if you miss the slot

Contactless Delivery

  • We offer contactless delivery options for your safety
  • Place items at your doorstep if you prefer not to meet the driver
  • Our drivers wear masks and follow health protocols
  • All packages are sanitized before delivery
  • Specify contactless delivery preference at checkout

Substitution Policy

  • If an item is out of stock, we'll suggest a suitable alternative
  • We'll contact you before making any substitutions
  • You can approve or decline suggested items via SMS or app
  • Same quality and price guaranteed for approved substitutions
  • Refunds issued immediately if you decline all suggestions

Product Quality Guarantee

  • All fresh produce inspected before packing
  • Quality guarantee on all items - expiry dates verified
  • Cold chain maintained throughout delivery for perishables
  • Full refund or replacement for damaged items
  • 30-day return policy on all groceries
