Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about orders, delivery, payments, and more

Orders & Delivery

▼

How do I place an order?

+

Placing an order with Snappy Fresh is simple! Browse our product categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, select your delivery location and time slot, and complete payment. Orders can be placed 24/7 and we offer same-day delivery for most areas.

What are your delivery areas?

+

Snappy Fresh currently delivers throughout Zimbabwe including Harare, Western Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, Free State, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, Northern Cape, and North West. During checkout, you can enter your postal code or province to confirm if we deliver to your area. We're constantly expanding our delivery network!

What are the delivery time slots?

+

We offer multiple delivery time slots throughout the day: 8-9 AM, 10-11 AM, 12-1 PM, 2-3 PM, 4-5 PM, 6-7 PM, and 8-9 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 6 PM. You can select your preferred time slot during checkout, and our delivery partner will keep you updated with estimated arrival times.

What is your minimum order value?

+

The minimum order value for delivery is R250. Orders below this amount will not qualify for delivery, but you may have the option to pick up in-store if available. Orders of R500 or more qualify for free delivery in most areas. Check our promotions page for current deals and special offers!

How much is delivery?

+

Delivery costs vary depending on your location and order value. Orders over R500 typically qualify for free delivery. For smaller orders, delivery charges range from R45 to R95 depending on your delivery area. Express delivery options are also available in some areas for a premium fee. You'll see the exact delivery cost before confirming your order.

Can I change my delivery slot?

+

Yes, you can change your delivery slot if you do so before your order is processed (usually up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time). Visit your order details in "My Account" and click "Modify Delivery Slot." If your order has already been packed and is out for delivery, we may not be able to change the slot. Contact our support team for assistance.

Payment & Billing

▼

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, EFT transfers, and mobile payment methods. You can also use third-party payment platforms like PayPal. During checkout, you'll see all available payment options for your region.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to protect your payment information. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways. We never store full card details on our servers. Your financial information is as safe as when you shop with any major online retailer.

Can I split payment between methods?

+

Currently, our system processes one primary payment method per order. However, you can contact our support team at support@snappyfresh.net to discuss special arrangements for splitting payments. We're constantly improving our features and this capability may be added in the future.

Do you store my card details?

+

We do not store complete card details on our servers. However, with your permission, we can securely save your payment method for faster checkout on future orders. You have full control and can delete saved payment methods anytime from your account settings. This feature is completely optional.

Can I get an invoice?

+

Yes! You'll receive an invoice via email immediately after your order is confirmed. You can also view and download invoices anytime from your account under "Order History." Our invoices include itemized details, delivery charges, taxes, and payment confirmation. For VAT invoices or bulk orders, please contact our support team.

Returns & Refunds

▼

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original, unopened condition with all original packaging and documentation. Fresh produce and perishable items have a 24-hour return window from delivery. Some items like medications and specialty products may have different return policies. Items purchased on promotion or clearance may have limited return options.

How do I request a refund?

+

To request a refund, log into your account and visit "Order History." Select the order containing the item you wish to return and click "Return Item." Follow the return form to select items and provide a reason. You'll receive a return authorization number and shipping instructions. Our team will review your request within 24 hours.

What if an item is damaged?

+

If you receive a damaged item, report it immediately through our website or call +263 782 978 460. Provide photos of the damage and the original packaging. You don't need to return damaged items for a full refund. We'll process your refund within 24 hours and may follow up to improve our packaging and delivery procedures.

What about substitutions?

+

If we don't have a specific item in stock, we may suggest a suitable substitute at the same or lower price. You can indicate your substitution preferences during checkout. If we make a substitution without permission, you can refuse the item or request a full refund. We always try to honor your original selections whenever possible.

How long do refunds take?

+

Once we receive and process your return, refunds are issued to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. Please note that your bank may take an additional 3-5 business days to process the refund. You'll receive an email confirmation when your refund is issued. For damaged items, refunds are processed within 24 hours.

Account & Security

▼

How do I create an account?

+

Creating an account is free and easy! Click the "Account" button in the top right corner and select "Register." Enter your email address, first name, last name, and create a secure password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for quick registration. Once registered, you can start shopping and track your orders immediately.

I forgot my password

+

No problem! On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. We'll send you a secure link to reset your password. Click the link in your email and create a new password. The reset link is valid for 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I update my details?

+

Log into your account and go to "My Account" → "Profile Settings." You can update your name, email address, phone number, and delivery addresses. Changes are saved immediately. We recommend keeping your information current for accurate order delivery and important notifications. You can manage multiple delivery addresses for convenience.

Is my information safe?

+

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Snappy Fresh uses SSL encryption, firewalls, and regular security audits to protect your information. We comply with international data protection standards and never share your personal information with third parties without permission. You can review our full Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we handle your data.

Products & Stock

▼

How do I know if items are in stock?

+

Each product page displays real-time stock information. If an item shows "In Stock," it's available for immediate order. Items marked "Low Stock" may sell out quickly. If an item is "Out of Stock," you can click "Notify Me" to receive an email when it's back in stock. Stock levels update hourly, so check back frequently for items you're looking for.

Can I request specific products?

+

Absolutely! We love customer suggestions. You can submit product requests through our Contact Us page or email support@snappyfresh.net. Tell us what products you'd like to see and why. Our product team reviews all requests regularly and we frequently add new items based on customer feedback. We're always expanding our product range!

What about expiry dates?

+

We take expiry dates seriously! All our fresh produce, dairy, and packaged goods are carefully inspected. We only stock items with at least 50% of their shelf life remaining. Expiry dates are displayed on product listings for your reference. If you receive any item close to expiry, you can return it for a full refund or replacement. Quality and freshness are our top priorities.

Do you sell organic products?

+

Yes! We have a dedicated Organic section featuring certified organic produce, dairy, grains, and other products. All organic items are clearly labeled and meet international organic certification standards. Organic products are sourced from trusted suppliers and are typically available for delivery like all our other products. Filter by "Organic" in the Fresh Produce category to see all options.

Technical Support

▼

The website isn't working

+

If you're experiencing technical issues, first try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page. Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge). Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, try a different browser or device. Still having problems? Contact our support team at +263 782 978 460 or email support@snappyfresh.net.

I can't place an order

+

If you're unable to place an order, check the following: Is your delivery area supported? (Enter your postal code to verify). Is your order above the minimum value? (R250). Have you selected a valid delivery time slot? Does your payment method have sufficient funds? If everything looks correct but you still can't order, contact our support team. We're happy to help troubleshoot the issue.

How do I use promo codes?

+

At checkout, you'll see a "Promo Code" or "Discount Code" field. Enter your promo code and click "Apply." The discount will be calculated and shown before you complete payment. Each code has specific terms (minimum purchase, product restrictions, etc.) which are displayed when applied. Check our Promotions page for current codes and offers. Some codes are one-time use only, others are reusable during the promotional period.
