Returns & Refunds Policy

Your satisfaction is guaranteed. Easy returns, hassle-free refunds

Return Window

30 Days

Return most non-perishable items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund

Fresh Items

24 Hours

Report issues with fresh produce, dairy, and frozen items within 24 hours of delivery

Refund Processing

5-7 Days

Full refunds processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method

24/7 Support

Always Available

Contact our customer service team anytime for assistance with returns and refunds

100% Satisfaction Guarantee. At Snappy Fresh, we stand behind the quality of every product we deliver. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, we make it easy to return items and receive a full refund. Your happiness is our priority.

1. Our 30-Day Return Policy

Hassle-Free Returns: We offer a 30-day return policy on most non-perishable items purchased from Snappy Fresh. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll make it right.

1.1 Eligible Items for Return

The following items can be returned within 30 days of delivery:

  • Non-perishable grocery items (unopened and in original packaging)
  • Household products (cleaning supplies, paper goods)
  • Health and beauty products (unopened and sealed)
  • Baby care products (unopened and sealed)
  • Pet care products (unopened)
  • Beverages (unopened bottles and cans)

1.2 Fresh & Perishable Items

For fresh produce, meat, seafood, dairy, bakery, and frozen items, our return policy is different:

  • Report within 24 hours: Contact us within 24 hours of delivery if items are damaged, spoiled, or not fresh
  • Photo evidence: Provide photos of the product and packaging
  • Immediate replacement or refund: We'll arrange a replacement delivery or issue a full refund
  • No return shipping required: Keep or dispose of damaged perishable items as instructed

Quality Guarantee: We handpick every fresh item and inspect for quality before delivery. If anything doesn't meet our high standards, we want to know immediately so we can make it right.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned once delivered:

Please Review Before Ordering: The following items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged except in cases of defect or damage.

  • Opened or consumed food items
  • Fresh produce, meat, seafood (after 24-hour reporting window)
  • Opened personal care and cosmetic products
  • Opened baby formula and food
  • Perishable items past their expiration date
  • Gift cards and digital products
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Clearance" on product pages

2.1 Defective or Damaged Items

Even if an item is typically non-returnable, we will accept returns if:

  • The product arrived damaged or defective
  • The item was past its expiration date upon delivery
  • You received the wrong product
  • The product has visible quality issues

3. How to Return an Item

Returning an item to Snappy Fresh is simple and straightforward. Follow these easy steps:

1

Contact Customer Service

Reach out to our customer service team within the return window (30 days for non-perishables, 24 hours for perishables). Contact us via phone at +263 782 978 460, email at support@snappyfresh.net, or through your account dashboard.

2

Provide Order Details

Share your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return. For damaged or defective items, please provide photos showing the issue and packaging.

3

Receive Return Authorization

Our team will review your request and provide a Return Authorization Number (RAN) along with instructions for returning the item. This typically takes less than 24 hours.

4

Package the Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging if possible. Ensure all accessories, manuals, and components are included. Attach the RAN label provided by our team.

5

Schedule Pickup or Drop-off

We'll arrange a free pickup at your address during your next delivery window, or you can drop off the item at our warehouse at 185 Lorely Close, Msasa, Harare during business hours (Monday-Sunday, 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM).

6

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email when the refund is issued.

4. Refund Methods & Processing

Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days 7-10 business days

4.2 Refund to Original Payment Method

By default, all refunds are issued to your original payment method. You'll receive:

  • Full refund for the returned item(s)
  • Proportional refund of delivery fees if the entire order is returned
  • Email confirmation when the refund is processed
  • Transaction ID for tracking purposes

4.3 Store Credit Option

For faster processing, you can choose to receive store credit instead of a refund to your payment method:

  • Instant credit: Applied to your account immediately upon return approval
  • Bonus incentive: Receive an extra 5% as store credit (e.g., $100 refund = $105 store credit)
  • Never expires: Use your store credit anytime for future purchases
  • Flexible use: Apply to any products on our platform

Store Credit Bonus: Choose store credit and get an extra 5% on top of your refund amount. It's our way of saying thank you for staying with Snappy Fresh!

5. Product Exchanges

Need a different size, flavor, or brand? We're happy to help with exchanges!

5.1 How Exchanges Work

  1. Contact us: Let us know you'd like to exchange an item
  2. Choose replacement: Select the item you'd like to receive instead
  3. Return original: Follow our standard return process
  4. Receive replacement: We'll include your exchange item in your next delivery (or arrange a special delivery)

5.2 Price Differences

  • Lower price: If the exchange item costs less, we'll refund the difference
  • Higher price: If the exchange item costs more, we'll charge the difference to your payment method
  • Equal price: No additional charges or refunds

6. Damaged or Incorrect Items

Inspect Your Order: Please inspect your order immediately upon delivery. If anything is damaged, incorrect, or missing, contact us right away for the fastest resolution.

6.1 Damaged Items

If you receive damaged items:

  • Contact us immediately (preferably while the driver is still present)
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We'll arrange immediate replacement or full refund
  • No need to return damaged perishable items

6.2 Wrong Items Delivered

If you receive the wrong item:

  • Contact customer service with your order number
  • We'll arrange pickup of the incorrect item
  • Correct item will be delivered free of charge
  • Option for refund if you no longer need the item

6.3 Missing Items

If items are missing from your order:

  • Check all packaging and bags carefully
  • Contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • We'll deliver missing items or issue a refund
  • No charge for delivery of missing items

7. Return Conditions

To ensure your return is processed smoothly, please ensure items meet the following conditions:

Condition Required Details

Original Packaging ✓ Return in original packaging with all labels and tags
Proof of Purchase ✓ Order number or receipt required for all returns
Within Return Window ✓ 30 days for non-perishables, 24 hours for fresh items

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

No, return shipping is free for all eligible returns. We'll arrange a free pickup at your address or you can drop off items at our warehouse. If the return is due to our error (wrong item, damaged item), we'll cover all costs.

Can I return items I bought on promotion or with a discount?

Yes, items purchased on sale or with promotional discounts can be returned. You'll receive a refund for the amount you actually paid, not the original price.

What if I lost my receipt or order confirmation?

No problem! If you ordered through your Snappy Fresh account, we have a record of all your purchases. If you checked out as a guest, contact customer service with your delivery address and approximate order date, and we'll locate your order.

Can I return part of my order?

Absolutely! You can return individual items from your order. You don't need to return the entire order unless you want to.

What happens if my return is rejected?

Returns are rarely rejected, but if an item doesn't meet our return conditions (opened, used, or outside return window), we'll contact you to explain why and offer alternatives. The item will be returned to you at no cost.

Can I get a refund if I changed my mind?

Yes! As long as the item is unopened, unused, in original packaging, and within 30 days of delivery, you can return it for any reason, including simply changing your mind.

Need Help with a Return?

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist with returns and refunds
