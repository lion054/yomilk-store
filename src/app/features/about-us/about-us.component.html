About Us

Our Story

Yomilk is dedicated to providing premium dairy products made from the freshest farm ingredients. Specializing in whole milk Every bottle of milk is pasteurized and homogenized to the highest quality standards. Yomilk aims to create memorable experiences with every sip, showcasing the richness of farm-fresh milk and dairy.

Vision and Mission

Vision: By December 2020, Yomilk envisioned becoming the leading provider of dairy-related products across the nation, known for its quality, consistency, and customer trust. Beyond national boundaries, Yomilk aspired to expand its impact throughout the Southern African Development Community (SADC) region, bringing their range of premium dairy products to a broader audience.
