Frequently Asked Questions

What are the requirements to become a vendor?

You need a valid business or products to sell. A business license is helpful but not required for approval. We accept both registered businesses and individual producers.

How long does the approval process take?

Most applications are reviewed within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your application has been processed.

What are the commission rates?

Our commission rates are competitive and vary by product category. We'll discuss the specifics during your onboarding call after approval.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are processed weekly via EcoCash, bank transfer, or other preferred methods. You can track all your earnings in the vendor dashboard.
